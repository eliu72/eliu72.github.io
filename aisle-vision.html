Aisle Vision

Contributers: Elaine Liu, Selina Hsu, Estelle Chung
My Role: Front-end developer

What if robots and store employees could work together to identify aisles that need re-organizing?

Our solution is Aisle Vision: a robot that checks store inventory and sends updates to an online platform for store employees. Using image recognition, it identifies when items need to be restocked - increasing the efficiency of store organization.
